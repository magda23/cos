About Organization:

Concept - Building Resilience of the Civil Society:

Tbilisi-based Caucasus Open Space (COS) is an independent think tank, designed to address the contemporary challenges facing the grassroots civil society, local and central governments, with special focus on the Eastern Partnership countries. COS is working based on the holistic and multi-disciplinary approach, with human security, sustainable development and political resilience at its core. Gender equality and human rights-based approach represent the cross-cutting elements of our work.

Topics:

Some of the areas of our work include:

  • Political Process & Resilience;
  • Economic Security and Resilience;
  • Information & Cyber Security;
  • Environment Protection & Energy Security;
  • Infrastructure and Human-Oriented Urban Policy;
  • Cultural Heritage & Dialogue;
  • Effective & Human-Oriented Local and Central Governance.

Some of the areas of our work include:

  • Applied research for evidence-based policy-making;
  • Drafting polity papers and recommendations;
  • Policy dialogue and advocacy;
  • Trainings, consultancy;
  • Technical support;
  • Networking;
  • Capacity building.
